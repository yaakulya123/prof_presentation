Prof. (Dr.) V. Balakista Reddy, the Chairman of Telangana Higher Education Council (TGCHE), is a renowned Professor of International Law, he served as In-charge Vice Chancellor of JNTUH and NALSAR University of Law and former Dean of the School of Law at Mahindra University and Registrar, NALSAR University of Law.

He led various centres on Aerospace and Defence Laws, Tribal and Land Rights, and SAARC Law. Dr. Reddy holds an LL.M in International Law from Osmania University, along with an M.Phil and Ph.D in International Air and Space Law from Jawaharlal Nehru University, New Delhi.

He also has Teaching and Research Experience of more than two and half decade. He was conferred the Meritorious State Teachers Award in 2017 by the Government of Telangana. He was also conferred with NALSAR's excellence in research award for the academic year 2021.

Acclaimed for his expertise in International Law, Dr. Reddy has presented papers at various prestigious forums, including the United Nations. Dr. Reddy played an important role in hosting the 78th Session of the Nobel Peace Prize-winning Institute of International Law at NALSAR in 2017. He was associated with the Indian Space Research Organization (ISRO) in drafting Model Space Legislation and had contributed to the WTO discussions regarding globalization of legal education and its impact on third world countries. Further, he was engaged in drafting laws for the Government of India, and the Governments of Telangana and Andhra Pradesh.

As the Director of the Centre for Aerospace and Defence Laws (CADL), he developed Master's and Post-Graduate Diploma programmes dealing with aviation law, space law, defence law and maritime law. As the Director of the Centre for Tribal and Land Rights (CTLR), he supervised the drafting of several landmark laws in Telangana and Andhra Pradesh.

Prof. Reddy has served as a visiting faculty at many reputed institutions across the world, including universities in the USA, Canada, the Netherlands, Germany, France and Sri Lanka, as well as various Indian universities. His publications include articles in national and international journals and several authoritative books on Air and Space Law.

He taught numerous courses in international law & also guided more than 20 Ph.Ds.

Dr. Reddy's dedication and administrative acumen have greatly contributed to strengthening legal education and social justice in India.
