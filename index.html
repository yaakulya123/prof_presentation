Prof. (Dr.) V. Balakista Reddy is a renowned authority in International Law, with over 25 years of teaching and research experience. He has held several distinguished positions, including In-charge Vice-Chancellor of JNTU Hyderabad and NALSAR University of Law, Registrar of NALSAR, and Dean of the School of Law at Mahindra University.

He has led multiple specialized centers, including the Centre for Aerospace and Defence Laws (CADL) and the Centre for Tribal and Land Rights (CTLR), both of which have played a pivotal role in policy-making and legal reforms across India. Under his leadership, CADL introduced postgraduate and diploma programs in aviation, space, defence, and maritime law, while CTLR oversaw the drafting of landmark land and tribal legislation in Telangana and Andhra Pradesh.

Prof. Reddy holds an LL.M in International Law from Osmania University and earned both his M.Phil and Ph.D. in International Air and Space Law from Jawaharlal Nehru University (JNU), New Delhi. His accolades include the Meritorious State Teachers Award by the Government of Telangana (2017) and NALSAR's Excellence in Research Award (2021).

As acclaimed scholar, Prof. Reddy has presented papers at prestigious platforms, including the United Nations, and played a key role in hosting the 78th Session of the Nobel Peace Prize-winning Institute of International Law at NALSAR in 2017.
